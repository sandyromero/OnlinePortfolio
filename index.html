About

Hi, I’m Sandy, a graphic designer currently based in Topeka, Kansas.

I’ve worked in print production, typography, photography, photo editing, videography, vectorizing images, and more. I’m interested in design that incorporates art, intent, and expression.

I've had the opportunity to work on projects for Wells Fargo Center, LA Metro, LAWA, and Cushmand & Wakefield.

My technical skills include: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, After Effects, familiarity with HTML & CSS (why just look at this dazzling website*), and of course Google and Microsoft offices.

*On a desktop please; still understanding media queries.


Sometimes the design magic just happens serendipitously when I'm playing, and I get the credit.
