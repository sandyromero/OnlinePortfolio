About

Myself

I obtained my Bachelor of Arts in Film and Digital Media with a concentration in Video Production at the University of California, Santa Cruz.

I became more interested in graphic design after taking design courses my senior year. Immediately after graduating, I started my part-time business as a graphic designer working with clients such as Central Coast Oncology and Hematology in Santa Cruz.

Thereafter, I moved to Los Angeles to work as a video editor and producer for a small online tech company. During this time, I had the opportunity to produce work for companies such as Los Angeles Metro, Wells Fargo Center, and Los Angeles World Airports in addition to traveling to New York City, Chicago, and the District of Columbia to produce video work.

Most recently, I've moved to Topeka, KS where I continue to work as a freelance graphic designer and a teacher(!!!) close to family.
